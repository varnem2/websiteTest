Baked Beans Recipe
Ingredients:
1 2-lb. Can pork and beans
1/4 cup mustard
4 slices of bacon
1/4 cup ketchup
1 chopped onion
1/8 cup cider vinegar
1/2 cup brown sugar
Notes:
side dish
Instructions:
Mix beans thoroughly with onion, brown sugar, mustard, ketchup and vinegar. 
Pour into dutch oven and place bacon on top of beans.
Cover and Bake at 350 degrees at least 1 hour.
